About Power Circle

The Power Circle of EIES is a platform intended to promote and support the exchange of information on Power Engineering among members and other interested parties. The main objective of Power Circle is to bring the academic staff, students and industry to a common platform, and there by prepare students for careers in Power Engineering. The Power Circle is active in coordinating various activities that are instrumental and conductive to the achievement of the objects stated above, such as workshops, talks and presentations, seminars, etc.

Mission

Introducing new trends of power sector and help students to achieve successful careers in power sector by organizing different programs like workshops, seminars and presentations.

Vision

Preparing students for good power engineering careers by working as common platform between industry and university
