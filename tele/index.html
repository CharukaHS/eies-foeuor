About Telecommunication Circle

Telecommunication and Networking circle is one of the key circle of Electrical and Information Engineering Society (EIES), as most of the students tend to specialize in the telecommunication field as their career. The main objective of the circle is to gather students around the circle and share the knowledge regarding the field and acknowledge them in the current and future opportunities of the field. Telecommunication is a rapidly growing field with new technologies evolving day by day. Thus, it is very timely to share the prominent knowledge regarding new directions well as the available technologies. Accordingly, the workshops are arranged by the circle and that will be a great support for the students to gain knowledge about the field. Also the circle itself urges the students to participate in various competitions to make their knowledge updated and sharpen with the moving telecommunication field. Above all, another main aspect of the circle is to seek for the job and training opportunities for the undergraduates collaborating with the passed out senior engineers who are already established in the telecommunication field.

Mission

Our Mission is to create opportunities to gather knowledge and guide the students of the prominent possibilities of the Telecommunication and Networking field.

Vision

To become the heart of ingenuity in Telecommunication and Networking skills and strengths of the EIES
