About Software Circle

The software circle is a platform designed to represent the students specializing in software in DEIE and the software field.

Mission

To open up the opportunities for the students with the help of academic staff and the industry, to acquire the needed knowledge and befit to the software industry.

Vision

To inspire students about software engineering and to provide opportunities to develop their skills as best suited for the software industry.
